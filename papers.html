  3. Impurity-induced non-unitary criticality

    HH.Li, Kuang-Hung Chou, Xueda Wen, and Po-Yao Chang arXiv:2502.12469

    The paper starts from the problem that Po-Yao asked Xueda with the interface problem between two unitary CFTs. Then, Po-Yao send this problem to me (about 2023), and I find that the importance of the EP is as followings: EP can be constructed by SSH eigenstates, but one cannot do the reverse engineering. However, this EP-induced criticality is not well-understood so far, and we intend to do it by comparing conformal tower of two CFTs in the future.
