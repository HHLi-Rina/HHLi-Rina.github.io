  1. Impurity-induced non-unitary criticality

    HH.Li, Kuang-Hung Chou, Xueda Wen, and Po-Yao Chang arXiv:2502.12469

    The paper starts from the problem that Po-Yao asked Xueda with the interface problem between two unitary CFTs. Then, Po-Yao send this problem to me and I find that the importance of the EP: EP is always eigenstate of both Hermtian/non-Hermitian SSH model, but it is the ONLY one for nHSSH. Therefore, our impurity model gives out specific CFT structure under PBC. The OBC stuff is still not well-understood in this community. Also, the analyical CFT calculation is expected to be easy but uhnfound so far.
